Hi there, I'm Tiffany!

I work with people to visually communicate their brands and products. From a complicated tech product to a personal portfolio, I approach design projects with the same goal: to convey their purpose with clarity, precision, and humanity.

I have been designing since I was 12, but formally I'm trained at the York/Sheridan with a BDes in Design. Since then, I've worked in agencies, inhouse product teams, and now as an independent designer.

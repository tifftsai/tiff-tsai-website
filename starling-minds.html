Starling Minds

Starling Minds came with a large number of existing assets through different stages of their product evolution. A comprehensive brand audit was done and a consolidated + evolved visual language was created.

Starling Minds delivers digital mental health therapy for workers and organizations, from early intervention to return-to-work solutions, they have comprehensive digital programs to help. Their brand had a large body of assets across their product and internal/external communications that required visual re-alignment. A full visual brand audit was done to help them implement a consistent visual language across their platforms, as well as a full website redesign. The result is an impactful, clean, and unified brand that fully features their expertise in digital mental health therapy.
